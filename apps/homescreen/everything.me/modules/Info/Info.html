What is Everything.me?

Everything.me delivers everything about any topic, from the coolest and most relevant mobile sources.

Example:

Enter a singer (e.g Lady Gaga), and instantly play the videos, stream songs, download, checkout lyrics and concerts. Everything you need in one place.

How to use it.

Use it as a super-fast way to find everything about anything - in mobile format, and from the best sources.

Mobile optimized web sites

Everything.me prefers mobile optimized web sites (technically: "HTML5 Webapps") when available.

Everything.me also offers dozens of exclusive mobile mini-sites, that are not available anywhere else in mobile web format.

Our goal is to bring the coolest mobile web sites for anything you may need.

Location

Everything.me knows your location, so just type "Sushi restaurant", or "Gas station" - no need to add "...in san francisco CA".

What can I look for?

Music, News, Shopping, Movies, Local, Social, Games - check the shortcuts tab for more ideas.

Why do I sometimes get a "browser" icon?

Sometimes the best resource is a non-mobile web site. In such cases you will see a browser icon meaning it may not be mobile optimized.

Add to Home Screen

Make Everything.me available on your phone's home screen.

Tap the share button and choose “Add to Home Screen”

  • Tap the Menu button
  • Choose “Add Bookmark” or “Save to Bookmarks”
  • From your bookmarks Tap and Hold everything.me and choose “Add Shortcut to Home”
